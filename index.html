Gun Violence Heatmap

Putting interactive data at your fingertips so you can make a difference

More than 30,000 gun deaths occur in the United States every year.

Gun violence is a complex problem that has changes over time and across geography. We must understand the data in detail before we can formulate effective solutions. With this app we've made the tools for doing that deep analysis available to anyone.

Gun violence statistics vary widely across states and counties.

With Gun Violence Heatmap you can visually explore the nationwide data and interactively control the map, allowing you to narrow your focus all the way down to the neighborhood level.

Gun violence trends across the USA have changed over time.

With Gun Violence Heatmap you can analyze various gun violence statistics with time series data, interactively controlling the timeframes and relevant statistics you are interested in.
